Welcome to Drumkit!

Please find below the digital drum machine that allows you to exercise your finger drumming skills while having fun with the different percussion instruments and drum kits...


Kit Select

Under the 'kit select' menu you'll find 4 different drum kits each having different sounds and styles assigned to the 5 percussion instruments.


Main Play Area

Below the menu, where the dum pads are, that's where play area is and where the fun begins!
You can play the drums by tapping the pads, by using your mouse to click them, or by using the assigned numeric keys for each drum pad.
Under each instrument name you'll find the corresponding numeric key for every pad. Try tapping those keys and see what happens!
These keys were assigned in an ergonomic manner in order to facilitate any beat performace that you might be inspired to play.


Enjoy! ;)

Please enjoy finger drumming and before anything Have Fun!
